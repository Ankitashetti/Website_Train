Ensuring Passenger Security

Committed to providing a safe and secure travel experience.

Security Measures

  • Deployment of Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Government Railway Police (GRP).
  • Installation of CCTV cameras at major stations and in select trains.
  • Implementation of access control and baggage scanners at key locations.
  • Special patrols on vulnerable routes and trains.
  • Security helpline 182 for immediate assistance.

Passenger Security Tips

  • Keep your valuables secured and close to you.
  • Report unattended baggage or suspicious behavior immediately.
  • Always lock your luggage and use chain locks where available.
  • Ensure your mobile phone is charged for emergencies.
  • Use official apps and websites for ticket bookings.
