Your Safety, Our Priority

Ensuring a safe and secure journey for all passengers.

Safety Guidelines

  • Always carry a valid ID proof during your journey.
  • Avoid accepting food or drinks from strangers.
  • Keep your luggage close and securely locked.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the train staff or authorities.
  • Ensure you have emergency contact numbers handy.

Onboard Safety Measures

TOG takes the following measures to ensure passenger safety:

  • Regular sanitization of coaches and stations.
  • CCTV surveillance at major stations.
  • Presence of Railway Protection Force (RPF) personnel.
  • Emergency chains in every coach for immediate assistance.
  • Dedicated women-only coaches for added safety.
